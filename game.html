Real

Real environment. 3D environment that can make people feel the real conditional. And people will learn the physical distancing in the game. The objects is designed by 3dmax, people can use a keyboard to control the objects.

Knowledge

People can learn knowledge about physical distancing in covid-19. For example, in this game, the user needs to get coin into the next level, and there are some NPC near the coin, when the user near the NPC <1.5m, the system will have alert and basic knowledge provide for the user. And also, on the map, we design some indoor places, people cannot enter that place due to the 1.5 m social distancing.

challenge

In this game, the NPC and challenges are to continue rising. And the user can have a deeper understanding of physical distancing.
