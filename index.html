Physical Distancing

What is the physical distancing? Physical distancing means safetydistancing between people. And this distancing is meant to keep 1.5meters away from others wherever possible and avoid physical greetings such as handshaking, hugs, and kisses. Which is aims toavoid droplet transmission. And also physical distancing is also including the space in households, school, a workplace which is aimto control the indoor population. Furthermore, Learning social distancing can help people keeping hygiene and avoid the densecrowd.
